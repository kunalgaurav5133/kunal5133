ABOUT AMPERAGE

"AMPERAGE" is TECHNO-CULTURAL fest of ELECTRICAL AND ELECRONICS DEPT. of MSRIT, Banglore. It was revived in 2014 with the aim of providing a platform for the student community to develop and showcase their Technical Prowess and reach large audiance.
AMPERAGE is known for hosting a variety of events that include competitions, exhibitions, lectures as well as workshops with wide range of fun events.
