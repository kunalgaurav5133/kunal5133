ELECTROCHARADES

'Electrocharades' is a fun and playful combination of 'electronics' and 'dumbcharades', where we give the regular dumbcharades an electronic twist. Only teams of 3 allowed. In the first round, one member picks a chit with a component name and enacts it to the other two and the team who guesses maximum correct components within the time allotted qualifies for the next round. Round two, combines the complexity of the first round with pictionary. One team member has to the draw things 'indicating' the components ( but not the component or device itself ) and the other 2 members have to guess. The team with the maximum guesses in the time allotted wins.
